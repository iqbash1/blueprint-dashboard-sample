Methodology & about this sample

Two layers

Results — are Hawaiʻi's economy and living conditions improving? · Implementation — are the 17 Blueprint actions being completed? Every results measure carries the same metadata: definition, baseline, latest value, trend, comparison, source & freshness, data class, and (once approved) a 2030 target with calculated on-track status.

State standing views

A standing tab is included only where a same-methodology series exists for all 50 states and D.C. (here: BLS payrolls, Zillow ZHVI, Census Business Formation Statistics), so rankings are always like-for-like. Measures without valid national comparators (e.g., visitor arrivals) carry no standing tab.

Direction chips

Because the Blueprint does not yet define quantified 2030 targets, this sample shows direction relative to the Blueprint objective since the report's baseline (improving / worsening) rather than on-track judgments. On-track status requires targets approved by the governing body and a published, metric-specific trajectory methodology.

Data classes & production path

  • This sample: all three measures are Public — reproducible (BLS via FRED; Zillow research data; Census BFS via FRED; population via Census). Refresh is scriptable monthly.
  • The full system adds Public — analyst-coded (action status from bills and budgets), Partner-supplied (UH, HIDOE/P–20, agencies), and Action-owner reported classes — each labeled on the measure.

Known limits — disclosed, not hidden

  • Federal series lag one to three months (payrolls, applications) or one to two years (ACS, BDS survival data).
  • Seasonally adjusted series are revised; values shown reflect the vintage on the "as of" date.
  • Zillow ZHVI is a modeled home-value index — methodologically consistent across states, but not a government statistic; it is used because no federal series publishes a monthly all-state home-value level.
  • Business applications count filings, not operating businesses; the full system pairs them with actual employer formations and survival.
